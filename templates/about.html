About

  • BetterYear is essentially a streamlined digital journal
  • "It is a tool to help you reflect on your life" - Github Copilot
  • You login to your google account, select a timezone, and fill in a form
  • A report is generated from the form, and stored in your google drive
  • Additionally, google tasks are generated based on optional goals you fill out

Why BetterYear?

  1. I type faster than I write.
  2. I have enough paper to keep track of.
  3. I have not been able to find an app that meets my specific needs,
    so I made my own.

Google Account Permissions

  • BetterYear leverages Google's software powerhouse to organize journal entries
  • This website stores nothing, except a google auth token in your browser's cache
  • Here are references for clearing the browser cache for chrome and firefox
  • However, your data is stored in your google drive, calendar, and tasks

Using Your Google Data

  • Birthday - make a time landmark specific to you
  • Tasks - organize lists of macro and micro goals
  • Drive - store your reports
  • Calendar - help keep track of when you reported what
